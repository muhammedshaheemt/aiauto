Quick Answers to Common Questions

Browse the most common questions business owners ask about automation.

How much time does automation actually save?

Most clients save 20+ hours per week after full implementation. In the first week alone, you'll save at least 2 hours from your very first automation. Over 90% of our clients report significant time savings within the first 14 days.

Do I need to be tech-savvy?

Not at all. We handle everything from setup to maintenance. If you can send an email, you can use our automations. We provide full training for you and your team, and ongoing support whenever you need it.

What if the automation breaks?

We monitor all automations 24/7 and fix issues before you even notice them. If something does go wrong, we have same-day fixes guaranteed. Our systems are built with redundancy and fail-safes to minimize any disruption.

How quickly will I see results?

You'll save your first 2 hours in Week 1. Most clients see significant time savings (10+ hours/week) by Week 2, and full 20+ hours/week savings by Week 4 after complete implementation and team training.

Can automation work with my existing tools?

Yes! We integrate with virtually every major platform — CRMs, email, calendars, accounting software, e-commerce platforms, and more. If your tool has an API, we can connect it. If it doesn't, we'll find a creative workaround.

How much does custom automation cost?

Pricing is customized to your specific needs. We start with a free strategy call to understand your workflow, then provide a tailored quote. Most single-process automations start at a one-time fee, while full business upgrades are flexible monthly investments.

What if I want to make changes later?

We build all automations to be flexible and adaptable. Plans can be adjusted, upgraded, or scaled as your business grows. Changes are typically implemented within 24-48 hours. We're here to evolve with you.

Is this just another subscription I'll forget about?

Not at all. Unlike passive SaaS subscriptions, our automations actively save you time every single day. You'll notice the difference immediately — fewer manual tasks, faster workflows, and reclaimed hours. Plus, we provide ongoing optimization to ensure you're always getting maximum value.
